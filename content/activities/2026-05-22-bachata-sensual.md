On the occasion of the International Day of Light, join us for a special seminar given by Fernando Ramiro Manzano (ITQ UPV-CSIC, Valencia, Spain), exploring the fascinating connection between wave physics, light, and dance.

Discover how the choreography of wave physics emerges from light-driven micro-objects to the analysis of partner dance, revealing unexpected links between photonics, dance, and the harmony of nature.
